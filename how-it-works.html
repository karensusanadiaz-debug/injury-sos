THE MODEL

A coordination layer that sits beside your firm — not another platform to run.

Personal-injury cases stall in the same places: a client who misses appointments, a gap in treatment that weakens the record, and weeks lost chasing providers for bills and records. Injury SOS removes that administrative weight so your team can focus on the claim. We coordinate care logistics on the provider you or your client already chose — we never select, rank, or recommend — and return a complete, organized record to your file.

1

One simple intake form — name, contact, accident type, and the provider already chosen. Attorneys and injury marketers can both submit in under two minutes.

2

Records & bills

Questions

How it works — FAQ

How much does Injury SOS cost a law firm?
Medical-care coordination is free to your firm. We are never paid by the firm or the client for coordination. Funding access is a separate, independently operated service.
Does Injury SOS choose the medical provider?
No. We never select, rank, or recommend providers. You or your client choose the provider, and we coordinate the logistics around that choice.
What does my firm have to do?
Submit a client through one short intake form. From there a single coordinator handles scheduling, reminders, gap-in-care follow-up, and records and bills retrieval — and you track it all in the portal.
Will the case data sync to our case-management system?
Yes, status updates sync to your CMS where supported, so your team sees treatment and records milestones without leaving their existing workflow.
Does Injury SOS work across Florida?
Yes. We coordinate cases statewide, including Miami, Orlando, Tampa, Jacksonville, and Fort Lauderdale.
