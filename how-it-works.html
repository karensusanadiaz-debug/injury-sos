Questions

How it works — FAQ

How much does Injury SOS cost a law firm?
Medical-care coordination is free to your firm. We are never paid by the firm or the client for coordination. Funding access is a separate, independently operated service.
Does Injury SOS choose the medical provider?
No. We never select, rank, or recommend providers. You or your client choose the provider, and we coordinate the logistics around that choice.
What does my firm actually have to do?
Submit a client through one short intake form. A single coordinator then handles scheduling, reminders, gap-in-care follow-up, and records and bills retrieval — and you track it all in the portal.
Will case data sync to our case-management system?
Yes, status updates sync to your CMS where supported, so your team sees treatment and records milestones without leaving their existing workflow.
Does Injury SOS work across Florida?
Yes. We coordinate cases statewide, including Miami, Orlando, Tampa, Jacksonville, and Fort Lauderdale.
